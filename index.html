Our Story

Born From The Elements

We started APEX Headwear because we were tired of compromising. We wanted the style of a premium streetwear hat, but the technical performance of top-tier outdoor gear. Nothing existed.

So, we built it. We spent three years sourcing aerospace-grade lightweight polymers, developing our proprietary waterproof coating, and engineering a floatable core that doesn't sacrifice breathability.

The result is a hat that looks just as good on the boat as it does at the 19th hole. A hat built to outlast the elements, and look damn good doing it.
